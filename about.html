THE CITY OF PUERTO IGUAZU

Puerto Iguazu is a city located in Argentina. It is one of the largest cities as according to the 2010 population census. Puerto Iguazu has warm temperatures during the winter and its hot during summer. It is alwayswet with no dry season. It rain probably every month. November has the most rain and precipitation falls during strong storms. The hot seasons of the city last up to 6months and more. the temperatures can reach up to 30 degrees celcus and 35 degrees celcus. During the night, temperatures go up to 18 degrees celcus and 24 degrees celcus. The cold seasons begin from late April to september with the temperatures of 21 degrees celcus and 11 degrees celcus. During some winters, there is frost but it does not happen regularly.


Puerto Iguazu is centered around tourism therefore there are a lot of hotels mainly constructed along the river banks of the Iguazu river. This creates a lot of jobs. The other tourist attractions include the three frontiers, La Aripuca resort, museum of images, Mborore Museum, Luis Honorio Rolon Municipal Nature Park, the Amerian Portal Del Iguazu Guira Oga center for bird rehabilitation, the hotel esturion, Iguazugrand hotel and casino, sheraton iguazu resort &spa. There is also the nearby wanda mines.

The tancredo nevels international brige connects the puerto iguazu with the brazilian border. They travel with taxis and minibusses. On the ecology section, it has an Atlantic forest. The subtropical forest has bamboos and a lot of birds. The are also lots of birds.
